Mission Statement

To inspire connection with nature through outdoor experiences in the Blue Ridge Mountains. We are committed to providing safe, inclusive, and unforgettable hiking, climbing, and camping adventures that foster a deep appreciation for the wilderness while promoting sustainable exploration for all skill levels.



Meet the Team

Lena Rivera - Lead Guide & Founder

Lena, the founder of Pine & Peak Adventures, is a seasoned guide with over a decade of experience exploring the Blue Ridge Mountains. Passionate about sharing nature’s wonders, she designs inclusive, thrilling tours that inspire adventure. Certified in wilderness safety, Lena’s leadership ensures every hike and campout is unforgettable, rooted in her love for the outdoors.

Tyrone Ellis - Rock Climbing Instructor

Tyrone brings energy and skill to Pine and Peak’s climbing adventures. A certified climber with years scaling Blue Ridge cliffs, he guides beginners and pros alike with safety and enthusiasm. Tyrone’s infectious passion for conquering heights makes every ascent a thrilling, confidence-building experience in the mountains.

Maya Nguyen - Sustainability Officer

Maya champions eco-conscious adventures at Pine and Peak. With a background in environmental science, she ensures our tours respect the Blue Ridge ecosystem through sustainable practices like zero-waste camping and local partnerships. Maya’s dedication to preserving nature inspires guests to explore responsibly while enjoying the mountains’ beauty.

Pine and Peak Adventures Sustainability Commitment

At Pine and Peak Adventures, we are dedicated to preserving the Blue Ridge Mountains’ natural beauty. Our commitment includes eco-friendly practices like zero-waste camping, low-impact hiking, and partnering with local conservation groups. Led by our Sustainability Officer, Maya Nguyen, we ensure every adventure respects the environment, empowering guests to explore responsibly while protecting the wilderness for future generations.
