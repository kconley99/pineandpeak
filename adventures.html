Adventure Packages

Explore the Blue Ridge Mountains with Pine and Peak Adventures’ three signature packages! Each adventure promises thrilling moments and unforgettable memories tailored for all skill levels. Choose your journey and let the mountains call you!



Blue Ridge Sunrise Hike

Embark on a guided dawn trek through the Blue Ridge Mountains, chasing the first light over breathtaking vistas. Perfect for all skill levels, this half-day hike includes expert insights into local flora and fauna, with sunrise views that’ll leave you inspired. Gear and snacks provided—start your day with adventure!

Difficulty: Easy–Moderate (suitable for beginners, some elevation gain)

Duration: 4 hours (half-day, starting pre-dawn)

Price: $75 per person

Wild Ridge Rock Climbing Experience

Scale the rugged cliffs of the Blue Ridge with our expert-led rock climbing adventure! Tailored for beginners and seasoned climbers, this thrilling half-day experience offers safe, guided ascents with stunning mountain views. All equipment included—just bring your courage and conquer the heights!

Difficulty: Moderate–Challenging (beginner-friendly with options for advanced climbers)

Duration: 5 hours (half-day)

Price: $120 per person

Forest Escape Weekend Campout

Unplug and immerse yourself in the Blue Ridge wilderness with our guided weekend campout. Enjoy two nights of cozy camping, expert-led hikes, and campfire stories under starry skies. Ideal for families or groups, we handle gear and meals, so you can reconnect with nature. Adventure awaits!

Difficulty: Easy–Moderate (accessible for families, includes light hiking)

Duration: 2 nights, 3 days (Friday evening–Sunday morning)

Price: $350 per person
